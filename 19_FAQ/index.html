How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also view live order status from the “My Orders” section in your account.

What is your return and refund policy?

We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. Once the returned item is inspected, your refund will be processed within 3–5 working days back to the original payment method.

Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?

Yes, COD is available in most cities. A small COD service fee may apply depending on location.

How can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled before shipping from the “My Orders” page. If the order is already shipped, you can apply for a return once it arrives.

Are the products original and authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% original and sourced directly from verified brands and authorized vendors. We do not list duplicates or fake items.
